The end of August is met with a brand new four track EP by
Blind Slime called "Dark Vibrations (In the Sky)".  Out now!

Dark Vibrations (In the Sky) is a free EP featuring four new tracks by West Virginia's very own Blind Slime.  Dark, liturgical moon-lit psychedelia written and performed by Jason Adams.
The title track (Dark Vibrations) will appear on the upcoming full length album "Mid-Atlantic Gothic", Blind Slime's follow up to his excellent "True Country" cassette.
Coming very soon to a forest near you...
 
(and did we mention that Dark Vibrations (In the Sky) is free?)
Yes indeed, so grab it now!
