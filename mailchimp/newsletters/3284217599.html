Blind Slime
Mid-Atlantic Gothic


Next Monday (January 21st) Wil-Ru will be releasing Blind Slime's Mid-Atlantic Gothic, a brand new full length album by Jason Adams.

Mid-Atlantic Gothic offers up 11 new tracks of weird, dusky funeral-esque psychedelic music that only a man who chose Halloween as his wedding day could come up with.
Spooky cathedral rock! Super highly recommended! Don't miss it!
Mid-Atlantic Gothic will be released as a limited edition cassette and also in digital format.

Download a free track from the album!
Blind Slime - "05 Withy Well"
Lamont Kohner - Willamette Dubs
(2 x CD - Album + Remixes)
The debut full length album by Lamont Kohner.

Willamette Dubs is comprised of five long-form atmospheric dub techno works and a full second disc of Willamette Dubs remixes by Ndru, Ohrwert, Grey Deer, Morris Arch Nightwork and Coppice Halifax (who also happened to curate the remixes), adding up to nearly two hours of pure  electronic bliss.  Headphones are encouraged!

This double CD is limited to 100 copies, so grab them while supplies last.

Willamette Dubs is also available in digital format via our digital
shop here: http://wil-ru.bandcamp.com/album/willamette-dubs

Blind Slime
Dark Vibrations (In the Sky)
In case you missed it, check out Blind Slime's free digital EP " Dark Vibrations (In The Sky)".  A four track precursor to Mid-Atlantic Gothic that features the title track, "Dark Vibrations (In The Sky)", taken from Mid-Atlantic Gothic along with three bonus tracks of dark psychedelic guitar driven instrumentals
Get it here: http://wil-ru.bandcamp.com/album/dark-vibrations-in-the-sky
