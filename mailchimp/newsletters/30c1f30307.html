David Tagg – WR027
(2 x Cassette)


Out now! David Tagg’s debut release for WR is simply titled by catalog number. WR027 is an insanely gorgeous remastered double cassette reissue of his incredibly limited edition release (25 pieces) formerly known as EEDCD-07. This reissue features nearly two full hours of pastoral ambient drone works that David constructed of synthesizers, reel to reels and field recordings. A bit of a departure from his more well know guitar based sound, but equally as compelling. A truly beautiful work of art! Don’t miss it a second time!

Remastered by the magic hands of Brian Grainger for your maximized listening experience…
