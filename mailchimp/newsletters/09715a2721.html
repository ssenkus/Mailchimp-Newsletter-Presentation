The DS-8 Drum Synth Clone PCBs are back in stock!
 
Our wildly popular clone of the Coron DS-8 Drum Synce circuit is back in stock with a totally awesome layout and redesign!

One of the coolest new features is the pushbutton trigger, which allows you to manually activate the awesome sounds with the push of an on-board button.  You still can use an external piezo or a synth trigger pulse, so you can adapt this circuit to any application you have!  Build a stand-alone synth or use it with your percussion set-up!

The RAT Clone
