8-step becomes 16


From Zach, a great customer and contributor:

"Synthrotek’s new 8-step sequencer strikes again!!
Connected the clock out to the CV2 input on the APC. Already had one CV-out connected to APC CV1-in. By imposing the clock pulse on the duty cycle, the APC doubles in “notes” it plays. It’s like having a fixed synced square wave LFO to play with.
Anywhoooo… Fun as always"

Thanks Zach!!!
